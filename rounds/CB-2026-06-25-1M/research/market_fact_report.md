## U.S. Macro Releases

- BEA reported May 2026 personal income increased $181.6 billion, or 0.7% month over month. Disposable personal income increased $164.9 billion, or 0.7%. Personal consumption expenditures increased $156.1 billion, or 0.7%.

- BEA reported Q1 2026 real GDI increased 1.2%, and the average of real GDP and real GDI increased 1.7%.

- BLS previously reported May 2026 CPI rose 0.5% month over month and 4.2% year over year, with CPI excluding food and energy up 0.2% month over month and 2.9% year over year.
- BLS previously reported May 2026 PPI for final demand rose 1.1% month over month and 6.5% year over year. Final demand excluding foods, energy, and trade services rose 0.8% month over month and 5.1% year over year.
- BLS previously reported May 2026 nonfarm payroll employment rose by 172,000 and the unemployment rate held at 4.3%. The next Employment Situation release for June 2026 is scheduled for Thursday, July 2, 2026 at 8:30 AM ET.
