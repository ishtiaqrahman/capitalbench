# Example Market Briefing

This is a fake briefing for a reproducibility fixture. It is written as if the
decision date were April 30, 2026, with a one-month holding period.

Equity markets have been mixed. Large-cap growth has regained momentum after a
soft start to the quarter, while small caps remain pressured by financing costs.
Energy has benefited from tighter supply expectations. Long-duration bonds are
volatile because inflation expectations remain sticky. Short Treasury exposure
is stable but unlikely to outperform risk assets if equities rally.

The model must select exactly one option from the supplied list. No portfolio
weights, leverage, short positions, or option combinations are allowed.
